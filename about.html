Welcome Message From

The Founder

A warm welcome awaits you at Mindfield School Excellence!

Embodying our motto "Elite the World", our mission is to deliver a holistic education deeply rooted in the teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ. By God's grace, we have cultivated a secure and nurturing educational environment where children relish positive learning experiences.
Born on September 24, 1972, in Dormaa Ahenkro, Mr. Ernest Oppong’s life journey is one of resilience, perseverance, and unwavering faith. Despite early challenges, including family separation and educational instability, he rose above adversity with determination. Starting from a humble table-top electronics repair shop, he built Ernest Oppong Electronics, laying the foundation for his entrepreneurial journey. In 2000, he married Mrs. Belinda Oppong, and together they’ve raised three exceptional children. In August 2018, driven by a passion for shaping young minds, he founded Mindfield School of Excellence in Dormaa Ahenkro—now employing over 60 staff. A devout Christian and a proud member of the Evangel Assemblies of God Church, Mr. Oppong continues to impact lives with faith, vision, and leadership. On the occasion of his 50th birthday, we honor a man of courage, faith, and purpose. Happy Birthday, Mr. Oppong – our founder, our hero.

Mr. Ernest Oppong

Our Uniqueness

At Mindfield School of Excellence, we embrace a distinctive Christian-based approach to education, where academic pursuit is seamlessly blended with character formation. We view the classroom as more than a place of learning — it is a platform for nurturing integrity, faith, and lifelong values. Our vision extends beyond academic success; we are devoted to developing our students spiritually, morally, and socially, empowered by the teachings of Jesus Christ and guided by our committed, highly trained educators.

Our Vision

Mindfield School of Excellence is committed to building a dynamic, Christ-centered environment where every student is valued, nurtured, and inspired to grow into the fullness of their God-given potential.

Our Mission

At Mindfield School Of Excellence, our mission is to cultivate a Christ-centered foundation for learning that nurtures every child’s academic, spiritual, and personal growth. Grounded in unwavering faith in Almighty God, inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ, and empowered by the Holy Spirit, we are devoted to creating a transformative educational experience.

In partnership with parents and guardians, we guide each student through a journey of self-discovery—celebrating their unique identity as a reflection of God's image and likeness. We are committed to helping every child uncover and develop their God-given talents within a nurturing and disciplined environment. Join us in shaping a generation that grows not only in knowledge but also in wisdom, character, and purpose—equipped to shine brightly in every sphere of life.
