Admission Policy

At Trinity Stars School, we are committed to maintaining a transparent and fair admission process that aligns with our Christian values and educational standards. Please read the policies below carefully to understand our guidelines and requirements.

Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must meet the minimum age and academic requirements for their desired grade level. We welcome students of all backgrounds who respect our Christian ethos.

Application Process

Complete the official admission form and submit all required documents by the deadlines specified in the admission timeline.

Parental Commitment

Parents/guardians must agree to uphold the school's values, participate in meetings, and support their child's education.

Interview & Screening

All applicants and their parents/guardians must attend a mandatory interview and screening session as part of the evaluation.

Code of Conduct

Students and parents must abide by the school’s code of conduct, promoting respect, responsibility, and Christian values at all times.

Admission Deadlines

Applications submitted after the deadline may not be considered. Please refer to the official timeline for important dates.

Fees & Payment

All fees must be paid according to the school’s payment policy. Late payments may result in suspension of services.

Policy Amendments

The school reserves the right to amend admission policies as needed, with prior notice to applicants and parents.

For more information or clarification on the admission policy, please contact our admissions office directly or visit us during working hours.

Withdrawal & Refund Policy

We understand that circumstances may change. Parents who wish to withdraw their child from Mindfield School Of Excellence must notify the administration in writing at least 30 days before the intended withdrawal date.

  • Refunds for tuition fees are prorated based on the withdrawal date and the term calendar.
  • No refund will be issued for registration fees or any other administrative fees.
  • Outstanding fees must be cleared before final withdrawal confirmation.
  • The school reserves the right to withhold transcripts and records until all accounts are settled.

Student Code of Conduct

At Mindfield School Of Excellence, we strive to foster a safe, respectful, and positive learning environment. All students are expected to adhere to the following principles:

  • Respect teachers, staff, and fellow students at all times.
  • Attend classes regularly and arrive on time.
  • Complete assignments honestly and with integrity.
  • Follow school rules regarding uniforms, behavior, and property care.
  • Use technology responsibly and respectfully.
  • Promote kindness and refrain from bullying or harassment.
  • Participate actively in school community activities.
