Welcome Message From

The Founder

A warm welcome awaits you at Mindfield School Excellence!

Embodying our motto "Elite the World", our mission is to deliver a holistic education deeply rooted in the teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ. By God's grace, we have cultivated a secure and nurturing educational environment where children relish positive learning experiences.
Born on September 24, 1972, in Dormaa Ahenkro, Mr. Ernest Oppong’s life journey is one of resilience, perseverance, and unwavering faith. Despite early challenges, including family separation and educational instability, he rose above adversity with determination. Starting from a humble table-top electronics repair shop, he built Ernest Oppong Electronics, laying the foundation for his entrepreneurial journey. In 2000, he married Mrs. Belinda Oppong, and together they’ve raised three exceptional children. In August 2018, driven by a passion for shaping young minds, he founded Mindfield School of Excellence in Dormaa Ahenkro—now employing over 60 staff. A devout Christian and a proud member of the Evangel Assemblies of God Church, Mr. Oppong continues to impact lives with faith, vision, and leadership. On the occasion of his 50th birthday, we honor a man of courage, faith, and purpose. Happy Birthday, Mr. Oppong – our founder, our hero.

Mr. Ernest Oppong
