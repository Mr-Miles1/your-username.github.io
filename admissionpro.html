Admission Process

Begin your journey with Mindfield School Of Excellence by completing our online admission form below. Alternatively, you may download a printable copy of the admission form to fill out at your convenience.

Admission Process

1. Get the Form

Collect the admission form at our front desk or download it from our website.

2. Complete & Submit

Fill in the form with accurate information and attach the necessary documents.

3. Review & Interview

Our team will assess your application and schedule an interview if required.

4. Admission Confirmation

Receive confirmation via SMS, call, or email upon successful application.

5. Pay Fees

Proceed to the bursary to complete payment and receive your official receipt.

6. Orientation

Attend our orientation to meet faculty and learn about our school policies.

Requirements & Eligibility

Before applying for admission, please review the following key requirements and ensure your child meets the eligibility criteria for enrollment at Mindfield School Of Excellence.

Age & Grade Level

Applicants must meet the age criteria for the specific grade level. Minimum age is 3 years for Pre-school, 6 years for Primary 1.

Essential Documents

Provide a copy of your child's birth certificate, previous report card, and two passport-sized photos upon registration.

Parental Commitment

Parents or guardians must attend an admission interview and agree to support the school's Christian-based values and policies.

Medical Record

A recent medical report or health declaration is required to ensure the safety and wellness of your child while in our care.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does admission open?

Admissions open every April and close by the end of June.

Are interviews mandatory?

Yes, both the student and a parent/guardian must attend.

Can I pay the admission fee in installments?

Yes. We offer flexible payment plans for interested parents.

Tuition & Fees

We offer competitive fees with flexible payment options to make quality Christian education accessible. Below is a brief overview of our tuition structure.

  • Pre-School: GHS 800 per term
  • Primary 1–6: GHS 1,200 per term
  • JHS 1–3: GHS 1,500 per term
